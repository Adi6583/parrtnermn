About Partners In Excellence

Transforming Lives Together

Welcome to Partners In Excellence (Partners), a center-based autism treatment center that has served the Twin Cities Autism community since 2001 and the La Crosse, Wisconsin community since 2010.

Our mission is to positively impact the life of each child through our unparalleled commitment and desire to see our clients reach their greatest potential.

Partners is recognized in the autism community for their measurable progress and results. We strive to maximize each child’s potential, continually monitoring progress to allow each child to become as independent and successful as they possibly can.

About Deb Thomas

Partners was founded in 2001 by Deb Thomas. Deb’s mission is to restore hope for the future of children and their families, whose lives have been touched by autism and other related conditions.

As a parent of three children, Deb found herself emerged in the special education community when her son, Matthew, was diagnosed with a physical disability at a very young age. Before launching Partners, Deb founded the Minnesota Center for Conductive Education in 1997 to help Matthew and others with similar diagnoses get the help and therapy they needed to increase their quality of life.

Through this program, Deb encountered an entire community of parents desperately searching for services for their children with autism. Partners has since evolved by hearing and seeing the needs of families in the community and responding to them.

Our Core Values

Partners In Excellence opened on January 1st, 2001 providing intensive autism treatment services to children and their families. Launched by Deb Thomas, after learning about the challenges that families face in securing high-quality services for their children. Deb vowed to bring strong values to serving the needs of others after experiencing similar challenges personally while she and her family sought optimal services for their son with a physical disability.

Our Evidence-Based Approach

Partners employs a multidisciplinary therapeutic approach to support children with autism and related conditions. By integrating Applied Behavior Analysis and Applied Verbal Behavior with Occupational, Feeding, and Speech Therapy, we enhance the effectiveness of therapy and improve skill acquisition.

Enhanced Communication

We help children build communication skills, making it easier for them to connect and interact with others.

Skill Development

Our therapy empowers kids to successfully learn new skills that boost their confidence and independence.

Sustainable Treatment Gains

We focus on creating lasting progress, so the skills your child learns truly stick.

Behavior Generalization

Children practice behaviors in different settings, helping them feel more confident and accepted in any environment.

Our Treatment Team

Each client at Partners has a core treatment team of therapists who provide the following functions:

Behavioral Therapist

Behavioral Therapists implement the individual treatment plan for the client with high energy and in a fun environment for the client, as to motivate the client to want to learn.

Primary Behavior Therapist (PBT)

Primary Behavior Therapists identifies and monitors client's goals to support client skill acquisition and ensures treatment implementation with clients.

Senior Primary Behavior Therapist (SPBT)

Senior Primary Behavior Therapists provide leadership and advanced clinical knowledge to identify and monitor client goals, ensure therapist treatment integrity, and support Primary Behavior Therapists and Behavior Therapists within the Pod.

Clinical Supervisor (BCaBA/BCBA)

Clinical Supervisors develop and monitor individualized treatment plans, collaborates with other to provide clinical expertise and strategic planning for client progression of treatment.

Program Supervisor (Supervise Pods)

Program Supervisors lead the day-to-day therapy operations including staffing, monitoring the client's case, scheduling, supporting family collaboration, and ensuring therapeutic procedures are implemented effectively for the client.

Mental Health Professionals (LPCC/LMFT/LICSW)

Mental Health Professionals oversee the overall picture of the client's treatment to ensure our therapeutic procedures are optimally benefiting the client and their family.
